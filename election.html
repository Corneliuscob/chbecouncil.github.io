Our main goal as co-presidents for the 2015/2016 academic year is to bring out the CHBE spirit by creating an environment in our department, which all students can identify themselves with. We want CHBE to become a community that stands out and we want CHBE to play as a team!

After 5 years here, we have done it all - from E-Week to volunteering to 8 courses a term to 5 exams a week as well as co-op work experience - the list goes on. So you can be sure that we know how things work around here. Along with our excellent Council Team, we are here to provide academic, social and professional development support. Ask us anything!

Contacting the Council

The CHBE Council values your thoughts and opinions on all our activities. Should you have any ideas or questions, please feel free to talk to any council member whenever possible. Our email addresses are also available on the Team page.
