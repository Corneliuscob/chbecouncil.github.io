Why are you a good fit?

We have both had the amazing opportunity to participate in the chemical & biological engineering undergraduate club (Sheida as the publicity officer and Pranav as the Vice President). Thus, we believe that we both have had some experience with the positions, objectives and the curriculum. Furthermore, both being international students, we have had a great exposure to various academic resources at UBC and have developed excellent communication skills to interact with instructors and students. Most importantly, we are enthusiastic to create positive changes and be committed to more than student responsibilities. Finally, since Pranav and I, have had many chances to work in groups, we have developed very effective and flexible team-working skills. This position not only would benefit our colleague students, but also will provide a valuable learning experience for us. As third year is anticipated to be a very challenging academic year, we are going to strive to assist the students to easier focus on their learning objectives and experience a smoother, more organised academic year. This is going to be achieved through devising a constant instructor-student feedback loop as well scheduling the exams, important assignments and events in advance and in a conflict-free manner. Additionally, this position will be a great opportunity for us as it enables us to get more involved in the academic curriculum and further strengthen our interactions with the associated professors and academic authorities.

What do you expect to accomplish?

As academic coordinators we both wish to give platform to CHBE students to raise their concerns with faculty members. Our plan is to work together and pursue the following responsibilities and duties:
1. Collaborate with faculty members to conduct various tutorials and workshops for CHBE students.
2. Develop and manage comprehensive academic support program
3. Conduct more interactive sessions like beef & pizza to allow CHBE students to discuss academic related issues.
4. Develop program for second year students to allow smooth transition from first year
5. Promote group studies in CHBE by organizing events involving students from each year.
6. To hold special tutorials for students before exam
7. To develop a platform for students and professors to raise general conerns anonymously
8. Organize programs for stress management during exams
9. Ensure frequent surveys for students to enable them to raise concerns
